What is Washify?

Washify is a new way to think about washing machines. Cheaper than rent-to-buy; better value than buying a low cost machine with a short lifespan.

We think a washing machine:

  • should cost no more than £15 a month
  • be repaired within 48 hours
  • be refurbished every 2 years
  • come with a free supply of laundry liquid
